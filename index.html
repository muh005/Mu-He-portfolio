About Me

Hello, my friends, welcome to my place, hopefully you enjoy it!

I am Mu He, an international student from China. You can call me Harmony. I spent my first eighteen years in Shanghai making so many friends and enjoying my life with my family. In those years, I feel people's sincere and harvest friendship.
